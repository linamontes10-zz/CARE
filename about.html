Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code is a seven-week long program giving young women in high school a background in computer coding and is aiming to get more women into the computer science job field. Each week consisted of a different type of software programs and topics surrounding computer science, such as Scratch, HTML, CSS, Javascript, Python, cybersecurity, and algorithms.The programs has helped us to make games, websites, robots dance, and blogs. During the last two weeks of the program, we are put into groups and put together a project that involves computer science and something that we are interested in, other than coding. The Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program has helped many young women learn more about and pursue computer science nationwide and is a great program to participate in.



C.A.R.E.

CARE is an initialism that stands for Coders Advocating for the Rights of Everyone that brings awareness to issues that needs to be heard. CARE provides general information and awareness on multiple topics that deserves attention. Big Business, Women in Sports, LQBTQ+ and Reproductive Health are the four main topics for CARE. Each one of us are passionate about these topics and we have provided credible sources, description and ways to get involved to each awareness. Every one of us worked on a topic that we are fervent about.
Faith, as a woman invested in sports and athletics, is eager to demonstrate and provide information about Women in Sports to ensure that the unjust treatments of female athletes is known to the world. With the Trump Administration, the rights of LGBTQ+ individuals are at stake thus, Lea is passionate to bring awareness to the disappearance of their freedom. Along with the mistreatment of LGBTQ+ members, the lack of attention the health administration has on reproductive health is absurd. This ill-treatment of reproductive health enticed Savannah to bring recognition and awareness to this crucial topic. The selfishness and the greediness of big business creates a harmful environment in which the safety of the public along with their health is at risk. Lina wants to ensure that the violation that big businesses make is known to others.
