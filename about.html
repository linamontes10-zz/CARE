About Us

Girls Who Code

Girls Who Code is a program that gives young women in high school and middle school an opportunity to delve into the world of technology and computer science. GWC has clubs that take place during the school year. They also host Summer Immersion Programs. These programs are aimed towards rising juniors and seniors. The program lasts for 7 weeks, with 2 weeks being dedicated towards a final project. As part of the Girls Who Code Summer Immersion Program at Adobe NYC, we created this awareness website by the name of C.A.R.E.



C.A.R.E.

There are four founders of C.A.R.E. We chose an awareness website in order to speak up about topics each of us is passionate about. There are 4 members working on this project, allowing us to have diverse awareness topics based on each person's desires. We speak up for everyone. We do this because we are the Coders Advocating for the Rights of Everyone.
