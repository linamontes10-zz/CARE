Description:

Reproductive Health has become a popular topic surrounding human rights in the past couple of years. With the possible loss of Planned Parenthood and the banishment of abortion in the future, women nationwide feel that their reproductive rights have been violated. In addition, if we lose funding to Planned Parenthood, numerous amounts of women will be neglected access to birth control, feminine hygiene products, and sexual education. Women should not have their own personal choices on their bodies to be decided on by conservative cis-white men who have no experience of "walking in a woman's shoes" whatsoever.

The links and events provided below bring forth the option to voice your opinion on reproductive health in a positive way. The contact information is given in the website's link below. CARE hopes that you will help reproductive health rights and issues gain more attention and help women gain access to Planned Parenthood's vital necessities for them nationwide through our helpful services.



Ways to Get Involved:

There are many things that can be done in order to support reproductive healthcare reform an dbring about awareness.
How does one "Take Action"? Attend an event supporting Planned Parenthood. Become a PP Defender: defenders are a core group of supporters who have said they'll give their time to defend Planned Parenthood, the health services they provide, and the people who rely on them. Change your profile picture to stay #IStandwithPP. Donate money to the organization; Participate in marches!
How does one "Join or Organize A Protest"?

  • Organize a core group.
  • Pick a time and place.
  • Round up volunteers
  • Make it known: put the word out through emails, posters and flyers, local media, word of mouth and any other way you can.
  • Organize speakers.
  • Think up a catchy chant.
  • Check for hazards or dangers.
  • Notify the authorities.
  • Organize permits.
  • Get equipped.
  • Check the weather.
  • Hand out information.
  • Clean up your mess.


How does one "Fight for human rights in your community"?

  • Join or start a local group.
  • Meet your politicians
  • Organize a Stunt
