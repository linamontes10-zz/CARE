Big Business Awareness

Slavery is the legal fiction that a person is property. Corporate personhood is the legal fiction that property is a person.
-Anonymous



Description:

Big businesses are the proprietors of many human rights violations. Abusive corporations are willing to take any mean necessary to stay in power, may that be bribery or deceptive marketing. While doing so, they are putting basic human rights in danger. Public health, the environment, and democracy are all exploited by big businesses.



Ways to Get Involved:

There are many ways to get involved to invoke change concerning the abuses of big business. The main method is to get educated, and spread your knowledge to others! Reading articles that inform the people on how big businesses rose and the influence they have on the government is crucial towards understanding the current abuses of corporations. Another way to get involved is to take part in petitions that are calling for change. Voting is vital towards reform in the United States democratic system. Donations are also helpful towards funding campaigns which aim to “challenge abusive global corporations”.
